About

REPL Studio is an online IDE made with REPLjs, which is based on JSJava.

I made this for my own learning purposes, but you can also use REPLjs (or repl-studio) for your own learning purposes, too. This is a tool for the people that want to make their own IDEs or for the people that just want to test out server-side languages in their browser.
